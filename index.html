About

The Cult Online is a multiplayer game that contains at least 2 teams, the village and the cult. The Villager's objective is very simple, which is to succeed every quest. On the other hand, the cult must sabotage the quests by failing them. There are 5 quests in total. If 3 out of 5 quests are approved, the village win, otherwise, the cult wins.
