About

Werewolf Online World Playoffs or known as WWOWP is a series of tournaments where players compete to be the only winner. The tournament starts off with 16 teams, having 10 players in it, and 5 substitutes. Only the winner with the most points will be moving on to the next level. There will be a total of 40 games before players move on. Each week, a new set-up is used so that players will not get bored using the same roles. Leaderboards will be released the moment the first team finishes.
